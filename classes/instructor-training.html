Très Barre Pro

There are many barre disciplines currently available in the fitness world. Many disciplines are inspired by ballet/dance, yoga and pilates however are lacking a strong fitness and anatomy foundation.

This leaves instructors with the tools to guide clients through a 60 minute dance workout however without the tools for identifying postural issues and modifying movements to suit all clients varying needs according to each client’s individual posture and alignment.

Founded by a certified kinesiologist, Très Barre Pro has a strong foundation in anatomy, kinesiology, proper postural alignment and form to give instructors the necessary tools to guide their clients toward optimal physical fitness.

Inspired by ballet, dance, pilates and yoga with a focus on cardio, along with strong anatomical and postural foundation Très Barre Pro provides instructors with the optimal training needed to not only guide clients through a safe and effective workout but also the possibility of making a lasting impact toward their optimal physical performance.

Level 1 Certification Pre-requisite:

Valid certification in ballet, dance, pilates, yoga, or group fitness instructor or personal training.
OR
50 hours attendance in barre classes with très barre pro certified instructor.

Level 1 Certification Requirements:

Completion of 30 hour course (4 days)
Completion of written (100 MC questions) & practical (teaching 1 hour class) exams.
10 volunteer très barre pro teaching hours
(at studio/location of your choice).

Level 1 Course objectives

  • Understand the basic principals of proper anatomical alignment and posture
  • Understand common postural misalignments and modifications for barre participation
  • Understand basic ballet terminology & technique
  • Understand basic pilates terminology & technique
  • Understand appropriate music selection
  • Ability to explain and demonstrate all Tres barre level 1 techniques and movements
  • Ability to modify barre techniques and movements for a variety of fitness levels
  • Learn to prepare and teach a 1 hour Très barre fitness class effectively targeting all major muscle groups, including strength training, endurance/cardio, flexibility and stretching for a well rounded full body workout.

Level 1 Course overview

  • Posture 101
  • Barre 101
  • Pilates 101
  • Ballet 101
  • Anatomy 101
  • Barre Class planning & implementation
